# AU Small Finance Bank Q4FY26 Earnings Call Transcript — April 27, 2026

**Management:** Sanjay Agarwal (MD & CEO), Uttam Tibrewal (Deputy CEO), Vivek Tripathi (Executive Director & Chief Credit Officer), Yogesh Jain (COO), Gaurav Jain (CFO, newly appointed), Ankur Tripathi (CIO), Prince Tiwari (Head of IR & FIG)

## TL;DR

- PAT ₹832 cr (+65% YoY, +25% QoQ), RoA improved to 1.8% for the quarter — management called out that this already meets the ROA level the Bank had guided for FY27, achieved a year early, but cautioned against extrapolating the Q4 print
- Credit cost guidance for FY27: management explicitly said not to model off Q4's 0.60% print — "we should build it around 90 bps" as a sustainable through-cycle number, prioritizing risk-taking capacity over further optimization
- Universal banking transition confirmed as filed: RBI waived the NOFHC requirement (conditional on no new group entity), final license application submitted March 2026, timing of approval unknown
- Long-term cost-of-funds ambition: CEO Sanjay Agarwal stated an internal target of cost of funds converging toward the prevailing repo rate (currently 5.25% vs AU's current 6.75%) once "mature" as a universal bank — explicitly flagged as a long-term aspiration with no committed timeline
- Credit underwriting described as self-regulating: management says growth will pull back "automatically" via underwriting model triggers if macro indicators deteriorate, rather than a discretionary top-down call

**Segment/qualitative color from the call:**

- **Deposits strategy**: four focused segments — Branch Banking (retail-focused, ~60% of deposits), Wholesale Liabilities, Government Banking, and Financial Institutions. Stability ratio (CASA + retail TD + non-callable wholesale) at 79%, stable YoY.
- **Growth vs. macro caution**: management stated credit will "automatically" pull back via underwriting model triggers if a market/product/customer segment shows stress signals — not a discretionary top-down decision.
- **MFI and credit card normalization** cited as the two segments driving the FY26→FY27 credit cost improvement.
- **CFO transition**: Gaurav Jain newly appointed as CFO on this call, succeeding prior incumbent; welcomed by IR head Prince Tiwari.
- **Universal bank transition**: RBI amended NOFHC applicability (waived unless Bank/promoter group establishes a new group entity); final license application filed in March 2026; no committed approval timeline given.

**Management tone / key quotes:**

## Signals

**Management is explicitly de-risking expectations after a strong quarter — a credible-management signal.** Rather than letting the Street extrapolate Q4's stronger credit-cost/ROA prints into FY27 guidance, CEO Sanjay Agarwal proactively anchored FY27 credit cost around 90 bps (vs Q4's much lower print) and declined to firm up universal-bank timing despite the license already being filed. This kind of guided conservatism, following a beat, is a distinct pattern from the more guidance-confident tone seen in Kotak Mahindra Bank's same-quarter call (see *2026 05 02 kotak mahindra bank q4fy26 transcript*).

**The cost-of-funds gap to universal banks (6.75% vs 5.25% repo, ~6.25% for mid-sized banks) quantifies the single largest re-rating lever available to AU.** A ~150 bps structural cost-of-funds compression, if the universal bank license is granted and liability mix normalizes over multiple years, would be a step-change to NIM (currently 5.65% FY26) — though management was careful to frame this as directional and multi-year, not a near-term catalyst.

**Self-regulating underwriting claim is a risk-management signal worth tracking against realized asset quality.** Management's framing — that credit exposure to weakening segments pulls back "automatically" via the underwriting model rather than discretionary intervention — is a testable claim; the next 2-3 quarters of GNPA/NNPA trend (currently improving: 2.30%→2.28%→2.03% GNPA over three quarters) will validate or contradict it if a macro shock (e.g., below-normal monsoon, per the sector-wide risk flagged at *kotak mahindra bank*) materializes.
