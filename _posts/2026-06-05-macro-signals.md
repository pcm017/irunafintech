### Key Geopolitical Development
- **US nighttime attack on Qeshm Island, Iran** (June 4): Major military escalation. US targeted Qeshm Island in the Strait of Hormuz. **Iran retaliated with missiles and drones targeting airbases across the Gulf.** This represents a significant intensification from the prior "port attack" characterisation.
- **Market impact:** Brent surged to ~$97+/bbl (3 consecutive days of gains before partial retreat); S&P snapped 9-day win streak (−0.74%); safe-haven flows; rising Treasury yields.
- **India impact:** INR "under pressure; rupee near record lows on oil/geopolitical shock" (collect note); crude import bill elevated; RBI rate cut space constrained.

### India Forex Reserves Structural Note
- India's gold share of forex reserves increased from 13.92% (Sep 2025) to ~16.7% (Mar 2026). Over 77% of India's gold now stored domestically (vs 59.2% a year earlier). Source: RBI Annual Report 2025-26.

## Signals

1. **US-Iran escalation most severe yet** — Qeshm Island attack and Iranian military retaliation is more serious than prior incidents. If Gulf airbases are sustained targets, shipping / energy risk premium rises further. India's crude import bill directly in the firing line.
2. **India decoupling from US selloff** — Nifty +0.47% even as S&P −0.74%. India market appears to be pricing in RBI MPC hold (stable rate environment) rather than tracking crude shock. Watch if India re-couples on a sustained Brent spike.
3. **RBI MPC decision** — June 5 announcement at 10am will resolve the immediate rate uncertainty. Hold expected; any forward guidance on liquidity or future cuts will be more significant than the rate decision itself.
4. **UPI FY26 milestone** — 228.3B txns (+30% YoY), 500M unique users — structural proof of India's digital payments stack maturing. Base effects will make 30% growth harder to sustain in FY27.
5. **ECB CBDC sovereignty framing** — Central banks are now explicitly framing CBDC as geopolitical tool. Relevant for RBI's digital rupee strategy and India's cross-border payment ambitions (GIFT City, UPI international).
