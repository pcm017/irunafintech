**Signals**
- PSU bank IBO recall confirms West Asia war has moved beyond financial market disruption into real economic disruption for Indian Gulf diaspora. If IBOs stay recalled, India's Gulf banking franchise faces a multi-quarter setback
- Global banking job cuts at 63,000 — the AI-driven workforce restructuring is real and accelerating, particularly in back-office functions (AML/KYC, credit processing, reporting). India's bank IT and BPO sectors (TCS, Infosys BFSI clients) are in the line of fire
- HDFC Bank WFH + Modi austerity = policy-aligned flexibility signal; could become industry-wide standard rapidly
- OpenAI's YC token investment ($2M per startup in API credits) is an extremely clever ecosystem capture: YC companies become OpenAI API customers from day one at zero incremental cash cost to OpenAI, while YC is incentivised to steer their cohort toward OpenAI tooling. **India startups in YC are now in OpenAI's financial ecosystem**
- Mercury $5.2B and Revolut S-1 preparation signal the global neobank/fintech IPO window is open — Indian fintech IPO pipeline (10 companies preparing) has global tailwinds
- ICICI Prudential Life ownership restructuring: Prudential stake sale could trigger a change-of-control dynamic; watch for domestic strategic buyer (Premji Invest? Kotak? SBI?)
