## Signals

- **RBI MPC June 3–5** — hold at 5.25% widely expected; but language on future path critical. Goldman's "2 more hike" call makes each MPC statement high-stakes.
- **Nifty PCR 0.49** — deeply bearish options positioning. Suggests retail/institutional traders are hedging downside heavily. Disconnect from Nifty spot recovery (+99pt Jun futures basis = mild optimism).
- **Anthropic IPO confidential filing** — when public, will be a defining market event for AI equity valuations globally. S&P record highs + AI IPO pipeline = risk-on regime.
- **India forex reserves declining** ($681.4B, −$7.5B WoW) — RBI spending reserves to support INR. At this burn rate, comfortable cushion remains, but trajectory matters.
- **LPG sales −24%** — demand destruction at consumer level from fuel price hikes. Households substituting or forgoing. FMCG and rural consumption outlook deteriorates.
- **Google–Berkshire equity deal** (AI capex) — signals that capital-as-commodity is the new competitive moat in AI era. Implications: cost of equity for AI companies rises globally; India's technology sector valuation frameworks will need adjustment.
