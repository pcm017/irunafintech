## TL;DR

- **RBI MPC June 5**: Repo held at 5.25% (3rd consecutive hold, neutral stance); capital inflow package announced — analysts estimate ~$5B/month; LCR-linked differential pricing proposed for bulk deposits.
- **Govt promulgated FII G-Sec tax exemption ordinance** — removes capital gains tax on FII holdings of Government Securities; Sahamati recognised as SRO for Account Aggregator ecosystem.
- **Operation XENOFISCAL uncovered**: SideCopy threat group using XenoRAT malware targeting Indian finance/revenue systems via phishing + fileless techniques; RBI has issued advisories to regulated entities for Mythos cyber threat.
- **India Inc CFOs top risks**: rupee depreciation, input inflation, softening demand — Nestle India chairman flags geopolitical churn on costs; small packs gaining as budgets shrink.

## Key Data / Quotes

### RBI MPC (June 5, 2026)
- Repo rate held at **5.25%** — 3rd consecutive hold. SDF 5.0%, MSF 5.50%. Neutral stance maintained.
- Capital inflow measures announced: estimated to generate **~$5B/month** in foreign capital.
- **LCR-linked pricing proposal**: Banks may be allowed to offer different interest rates on bulk rupee deposits based on the liquidity treatment of those deposits — a structural reform for deposit pricing.
- RBI held amid weakening rupee (INR/USD ~94.99–95.11) and West Asia conflict pressures.

### Regulatory / Policy
- **FII G-Sec Tax Exemption**: Income-tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2026 — FIIs exempt from capital gains on Government Securities. Aim: deepen foreign bond market participation post JPMorgan GBI-EM inclusion.
- **Sahamati as AA SRO**: RBI formally recognised Sahamati as the Self-Regulatory Organisation for the Account Aggregator ecosystem — formalises governance and standard-setting for India's account aggregation framework.
- **Canara Bank fine**: RBI imposed ₹41.80 lakh penalty on Canara Bank for non-compliance with RBI directions.
- **Expert view (Raghav Pandey)**: "The goal is not softer regulation, but smarter regulation that delivers timely outcomes."
- **HDFC Bank cleared**: Courts, RBI, and independent reviewers have found no material adverse findings. Persistence of crisis narrative raises questions about source and motivation.

### Cybersecurity
- **Operation XENOFISCAL**: SideCopy advanced threat group using XenoRAT malware, targeting Indian finance and revenue systems via sophisticated phishing and fileless techniques.
- **RBI on Mythos**: RBI is fully prepared for Mythos platform cyber threat; advisories issued to all regulated entities; awaiting details on India's access.
- **Anthropic**: Urging AI industry to coordinate on a mechanism to pause advanced AI development if risks grow beyond current understanding. Rival OpenAI favours government-set rules.

### Macro / Demand Signals
- **India Inc CFOs**: Rupee fall, input inflation, softening consumer demand = top 3 near-term risks.
- **Nestle India chairman**: Geopolitical churn impacting input costs.
- **Small packs gaining**: Consumer budgets shrinking; FMCG producers shifting product mix toward smaller pack sizes.
- **FDI equity inflows**: +18% YoY in FY26; US investments in India doubled year-on-year.
- **Defence ministry**: Doubled financial powers of field commanders.

### Fintech / Lending
- **Truhome Finance**: Received SEBI nod for IPO — home loan/housing finance company; signals continued investor interest in mortgage lending.
- **Honeycomb Insurance**: Raised $40M (insurtech segment funding continues).
- **Plaid**: Released "State of Intelligent Finance" report — will people trust AI agents to handle finances? Consumer trust is the key variable for AI in financial services.
- **Crypto exchanges → "everything exchanges"**: Exchanges expanding into lending, payments, banking, wealth management; lines between crypto and traditional fintech blurring.
- **Community banks path forward** (Alex Johnson/Fintech Takes): Existential pressure from digital-first competitors + consolidation; differentiation remains possible but difficult.

## Signals

- **$5B/month capital inflow target** from RBI package + FII G-Sec tax exemption = direct policy response to rupee weakness and CAD pressure. Bond market-oriented solution — avoids rate hikes that would hurt growth.
- **Sahamati as SRO** is the institutional upgrade the AA ecosystem has needed. Formalised governance = foundation for AA at scale. Watch for revised AA API standards and interoperability mandates.
- **Operation XENOFISCAL** is the most sophisticated state-sponsored cyber threat targeting India's financial infrastructure documented publicly. SideCopy (Pakistan-linked advanced persistent threat group) in Indian finance = escalation from prior SMB phishing campaigns. Banks and NBFCs need incident response protocols specific to fileless/XenoRAT-type attacks.
- **Anthropic's AI pause advocacy** is the first time a frontier AI lab has publicly called for industry-wide coordination mechanism for development pause. Regulatory impact: speeds up EU AI Act enforcement conversations, potentially triggers RBI/SEBI AI governance escalation.
- **FDI +18% + US doubling** = strong positive for India's external financing story despite CAD/rupee pressure. But structural net FDI may still be declining (profit repatriation rising) — gross FDI headline vs. net FDI watch.
