# Poonawalla Fincorp Q1 FY27 Investor Presentation

## TL;DR

- **AUM ₹67,054 Cr (+62.5% YoY, +11.1% QoQ); PAT ₹308 Cr (+391.5% YoY, +20.8% QoQ)** — the ROA-recovery trajectory flagged as an "inflection point" in Q4 FY26 has continued into Q1 FY27 rather than stalling.
- **ROA improved to 1.98%** (from 1.81% Q4 FY26, 0.68% Q1 FY26) — a full 130bps YoY improvement; management guides continued 35-40% AUM CAGR over the next couple of years.
- **Asset quality kept improving: GNPA 1.37% (-7bps QoQ, -47bps YoY), NNPA 0.70%** — the 8th consecutive quarter of GNPA improvement from the 1.84% Jun-25 peak.
- **₹2,500 Cr QIP raised in April 2026** — leverage (debt-to-equity) fell to 3.82x post-raise from 4.67x at Mar-26.
- New products (Prime PL, Gold Loan, Consumer Durables, CV, Education, Shopkeeper Loan) contributed ₹3,600+ Cr disbursement (26% of total) in Q1 FY27; Gold Loan branch network expanded to 460.
- Fee income (Cross-sell + Servicing income) ₹161 Cr, separately disclosed — not bundled into NII.

## Signals

Poonawalla's Q4 FY26 "inflection point" framing is holding up one quarter later — ROA improved a further 17bps QoQ to 1.98% and PAT nearly quadrupled YoY, with the improvement broad-based across NII growth (84.3% YoY), operating leverage (cost-to-income down to 44.6% from 57.7% a year ago), and asset quality (GNPA's 8th consecutive quarterly improvement). The April 2026 QIP (₹2,500 Cr) sized the capital-raise thesis flagged pro-forma on the Q4 FY26 entity page — leverage is now confirmed at 3.82x, in line with the pro-forma 20.74% CAR estimate carried since May. The one open item this quarter is NIM: this presentation doesn't disclose a Q1 FY27 NIM figure, leaving the wiki's tracked value still anchored to Q4 FY26's transcript-sourced 9.05% — a gap worth closing at the next call or transcript release, since NIM trajectory is the key swing factor in whether Poonawalla's ROA keeps compounding toward management's 35-40% AUM CAGR guidance.
