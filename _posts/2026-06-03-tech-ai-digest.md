## Key data / quotes

- **Anthropic IPO (confidential filing June 1):** $965B valuation; $47B ARR run-rate. Potential Wall Street debut Fall 2026. OpenAI also readying confidential filing. SpaceX also pricing IPO this month. Dotcom bubble comparisons circulating (Fortune). "Ratifies Wall Street's AI obsession."
- **Google–Berkshire equity deal (Stratechery/Bloomberg):** Google issued equity to Berkshire Hathaway for AI capex. Structural thesis: tech industry Phase 3 (capital-intensive) after Phase 1 (equity-raising) and Phase 2 (capital-returning via buybacks). "Capital is now the ultimate commodity." Alphabet raising $80B separately. Amazon/Microsoft/Alphabet/Meta collectively: ~$700B AI infra capex in 2026.
- **Nvidia AI agent stack (The Rundown):** Nvidia moves to dominate not just training/inference hardware but full software + orchestration layer for AI agents. Architecture: GPUs + NVLink + CUDA + NIM microservices + orchestration tools. "Be the AWS of AI agents."
- **Microsoft AI-native laptop:** First laptop designed from ground up with AI inference at hardware level. Dedicated NPU integrated into device architecture.
- **a16z Visual AI to Code:** Next frontier of visual AI = generating code that produces visual artifacts (not generating images directly). Enables control, editability, composability. "Code as creative medium."
- **India semiconductor VC momentum (ETCIO):** DLI (Design-Linked Incentive) beneficiaries raising private capital: Mindgrove Technologies, VerveSemi, InCore, BigEndian Semiconductors, Calligo Technologies, MOSart Semi. Indian chip design startup ecosystem gaining traction.
- **Gartner India cloud $17.5B (2026):** Infrastructure and platform services leading expansion. Organizations prioritising cloud-first for AI-ready infra.
- **India chip future (Public Policy):** Policy reasoning around ISM progress, DLI outcomes, and whether India's chip ambitions can survive competitive pressure from Taiwan and China foundries. "Cockroach problems" — persistent policy challenges that survive multiple reform attempts.
- **Palo Alto AI cyber warning:** AI enabling more sophisticated, faster, targeted attacks. AI-powered threat detection needed. Quantum: relevant for future encryption risk.
- **DriveNets $410M:** Network infrastructure startup raising amid telco AI transformation.
- **BenEvans:** Anthropic $47B ARR; "tokenmania" continues in markets; LLM pricing floor still being discovered as compute costs fall and competition intensifies.
- **ByteByteGo:** Engineering workforce splitting — those who use AI effectively vs. those displaced. Guide for "AI-native engineers."
- **PM trends 2026 (Amy Mitchell):** Bottleneck shifted from AI capability to organisational learning. "Builder PMs" who can design and implement AI features most valuable. AI acceleration real but uneven.

## Signals

- **Anthropic IPO** = most consequential tech IPO since Alibaba in terms of AI market structure signal. Fall 2026 timeline means H2 2026 AI equity markets will be defined by this event + SpaceX + OpenAI pipeline.
- **Google–Berkshire capital-as-commodity thesis** — implications for India: Indian IT services companies that compete on labour arbitrage will face growing pressure as their clients shift capex from human capital to AI infrastructure. Watch TCS, Infosys, Tech Mahindra capital allocation strategies.
- **Nvidia AI agent stack** — if successful, creates a CUDA-style lock-in for the entire agentic AI application layer. Indian tech companies building agents must decide: build on Nvidia stack or differentiate.
- **India semiconductor DLI traction** — private VC backing of DLI beneficiaries (Mindgrove, VerveSemi, etc.) validates India's chip design ecosystem. These are pre-revenue or early-revenue companies — the commercialisation path is long but the signal is positive.
- **AI cyberattacks (Palo Alto)** — AI-enabled threat acceleration is a new risk vector for Indian banking (already flagged in ETCIO). BFSI-native AI security tooling is an emerging product category.
