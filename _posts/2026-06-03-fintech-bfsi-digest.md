## Key data / quotes

- **UPI May 2026 (NPCI confirmed):** 23.2 billion transactions. Daily average: 737.79 million transactions. Daily value: ₹84,423 crore. +24% YoY volume, +19% YoY value. NPCI note: figures exclude Credit Card on UPI and Credit Line on UPI — actual volumes are higher.
- **India-Oman CEPA:** Free trade pact (Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement) comes into force. Oman = key GCC gateway. Bilateral trade growing. Textile stocks outperform on D-Street.
- **GST May 2026:** ₹1.94 lakh crore (+3.2% YoY). Slowing from April record. Import-linked revenues boosted while domestic revenue declined. On track for FY27 initial months.
- **West Asia war-risk premiums:** +5x spike on shipping/marine insurance for India-bound cargo. Direct impact on crude oil import costs and all maritime cargo insurance.
- **Truhome Finance:** SEBI IPO approval expected — housing finance NBFC entering public markets amid elevated interest rates.
- **RBI MPC (June 3–5):** Hold at 5.25% widely expected. RBI likely to revise inflation projections upward following fuel price hikes and food price pressures.
- **Corgi AI insurer:** $1.3B valuation on May 6 → $2.6B on May 28 (2x in 22 days) on only $40M of written premium. AI-native insurance underwriting commanding extreme multiples — potentially bubble dynamics vs. genuinely superior underwriting tech (unknown yet).
- **GenAI guardrails for Indian banking (ETCIO):** Global AI models fail for India-specific regulatory compliance and multilingual nuances. Indian banks need BFSI-native guardrail frameworks. India cloud (Gartner): $17.5B in 2026 (AI-ready infrastructure demand).
- **BIS stablecoin research:** Minimum reserve requirements can stabilise stablecoin issuers' portfolios; tradeoff between stability and yield at regulatory boundary.
- **Andrew Bailey (BoE) speech:** "Can AI make cutlery?" — AI's intersection with economic productivity and monetary policy transmission.
- **SEBI Ashwani Bhatia → ICICI Bank:** Former SEBI Whole-Time Director joins ICICI Bank as Additional Independent Director — regulatory–industry talent flow.
- **Suzlon:** SEBI investigation resulted in ₹29 crore fine for accounting irregularities.
- **India RAF deal:** India issued formal request to France for 114 Rafales (₹3.25L cr deal); French reply expected in 2–3 months.
- **India-Myanmar:** Staying in touch on critical minerals and rare earths.

## Signals

- **UPI ATH + India-Oman FTA** — structural digital payments maturity + GCC trade corridor opening simultaneously. Combined: UPI expansion into GCC remittance corridors (India-Oman, India-UAE) is a long-term payments opportunity.
- **War-risk premiums 5x** — direct shipping cost increase for Indian importers. Not yet in WPI/CPI data; will appear in June/July trade deficit figures. Watch June trade deficit release.
- **Corgi AI insurer valuation** — canary for AI insurance underwriting. If genuinely superior (better claims prediction, adverse selection avoidance), traditional insurer loss ratios will deteriorate vs. AI-native competitors. India: policy context ahead of 100% FDI insurance.
- **GenAI banking guardrails gap** — India-specific challenge that creates a product opportunity for Indian AI startups building BFSI-native compliance/validation tools.
- **Truhome IPO** — housing finance NBFC IPO in an elevated rate environment = market test for the sector after SBI Cards, Bajaj Finance data sheet.
