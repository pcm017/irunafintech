## Signals

- **Bank fraud ₹48,021 Cr in FY26** is a system-level risk signal — this is among the highest quantum ever reported; AI-enabled fraud (deepfakes, synthetic KYC) likely driving the increase; directly impacts provisioning requirements and credit costs for PSU and private banks
- **Kotak NIM compression guidance** — when Kotak (historically disciplined on NIMs) signals sector-wide pressure, it's credible; Q1 FY27 results across private banks likely to show spread compression; watch HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, ICICI Bank Q1 guidance
- **Anthropic Mythos + Bank of Italy** — marks a new phase in AI governance: specific AI capabilities triggering sovereign-level regulatory responses; India's RBI/SEBI will be watching; financial sector AI regulation is coming faster than expected
- **AI venture era ending** — The Ken's framing is structurally important for India's startup ecosystem; fintech startups that raised on AI narratives without path to profitability face a tougher funding environment; valuation resets likely for pre-revenue AI-first fintechs
- **Gold demand −70%** — the customs duty hike (6%→15%) has crushed domestic consumer demand; RBI's gold reserve strategy (buying at market) is separate from consumer; but the demand collapse will hurt jewellers, gold loan NBFCs' disbursement growth, and gold refinery economics
