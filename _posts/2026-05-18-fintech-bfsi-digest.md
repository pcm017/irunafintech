## Signals

1. **BaaS fragility is the structural lesson from Synapse.** India is building a co-lending and BaaS ecosystem (OCEN, Account Aggregator, fintech-bank co-lending partnerships). Synapse's failure — a middleware layer trusted by both banks and fintechs — is a direct cautionary tale. RBI's co-lending framework and AA ecosystem need contingency planning for middleware failure.

2. **AI cybersecurity at banks** — a community bank AI incident in the US is the first documented "AI-induced" breach (vs. AI-assisted breach). Indian PSBs adopting AI (FinMin cybersecurity mandate) need to segment AI risk from traditional cyber risk in their frameworks.

3. **PayPal fair lending** — as embedded credit expands (BNPL, wallet-linked credit, QR-based merchant loans), fair lending obligations extend to non-traditional lenders. India equivalent: RBI's fair practices code + FLDG rules for fintech-bank co-lending.

4. **Adani SEC = institutional capital unlock for India infra.** Quantifying impact: Adani's debt market access in US (bonds, institutional term loans) could open $10–20B+ in new financing capacity for the group's infrastructure pipeline. Watch for: Adani Green, Adani Ports bond issuances in USD within 90 days of settlement.
