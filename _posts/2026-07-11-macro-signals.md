## Signals

**Change detection:** India's options market has flipped from complacent to cautious for the first time since the June rally began — VIX up to 14.54 and PCR down to 0.61, a clean reversal of the low-VIX/bullish-PCR combination that persisted through late June and early July.

**Root cause traversal:** This is a direct read-through of the Iran ceasefire breakdown (Jul 8) and the Gulf-state missile strikes (Jul 9) — not a standalone India event. Brent's weekly gain of ~4.7% is the clearest single data point tying the options-market shift to the geopolitical trigger.

**Sizing / impact:** If this VIX/PCR shift persists into the August 3-5 RBI MPC, it complicates the rate-cut thesis built on CPI below target plus cheap crude. The conflicting India-forex-reserves data flagged on Jul 10 remains unresolved this cycle (not refetched).
