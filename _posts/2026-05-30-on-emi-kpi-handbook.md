## TL;DR

KPI handbook confirms the Q4 FY26 metrics from the earnings presentation and adds critical operating detail. Key insights: (1) Avg. tenure jumped to 16.72 months in Q4 (+31% YoY) — meaningful cohort extension, improving LTV but also extending credit risk. (2) Repeat customer AUM share fell from 73.3% to 49.5% YoY — growth is now predominantly new customer acquisition, a different risk profile. (3) Spread improved sharply to 19.3% in Q4 as cost of borrowings fell to 13.3% (from 14.9% YoY) — cost of funds tailwind visible. (4) Cost-to-income rising (59.1% in Q4 vs 54.6% in Q4 FY25) — opex growth outpacing income, mostly driven by LAP expansion (1,084 LAP staff, +108% YoY).
