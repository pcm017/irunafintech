## TL;DR

- **Google-SpaceX compute deal: $920M/month** for 110,000 NVIDIA GPUs through mid-2029 — one of the largest compute procurement deals in history; signals AI infrastructure at sovereign-scale.
- **Samsung to invest $4B in Vietnam chip-testing plant** — semiconductor manufacturing footprint diversification from Korea/China to Southeast Asia.
- **Anthropic urges AI development pause** coordination mechanism among frontier labs if risks grow; first frontier lab to publicly call for such coordination.
- **AirTrunk commits $30B in India data centers** — largest single data center investment commitment to India, driven by AI workload demand.

## Key Data / Quotes

### Big Tech Strategy
- **Google + SpaceX compute deal**: $920M/month for computing power through mid-2029 — ~110,000 NVIDIA GPUs + other hardware. Covers surging AI product demand. One of the largest single compute procurement deals ever. Context: Google also raised equity from Berkshire Hathaway for AI infrastructure (logged prior weeks).
- **Google Cloud layoffs**: Google laid off employees in Cloud division — AI-driven restructuring as teams reorganise around AI product priorities.
- **Microsoft metered intelligence**: Building for a world of consumption-based AI pricing as infrastructure costs demand sustainable unit economics. Moving from flat-rate to metered AI APIs.
- **Adobe CEO search**: Adobe looking at David Wadhwani and Anil Chakravarthy as internal candidates + external search. Comes as Adobe faces growing competition from AI-native design/creative tools.
- **Bitcoin fading**: Bitcoin values declining as investors shift to AI stocks and megacap IPOs (SpaceX, Anthropic, OpenAI pipeline). Capital rotation from crypto to AI equity is accelerating.

### AI Safety / Regulation
- **Anthropic AI development pause**: Anthropic urging top AI firms to coordinate on a mechanism to pause advanced AI development if risks grow. Rapid improvements could lead to humans losing control. Rival OpenAI: governments should set rules. This is Anthropic's first public call for coordinated pause mechanism. Signals: frontier AI safety conversation escalating from internal to industry-wide.

### AI Research
- **Continual learning** (Tanay): The key frontier for AI agents is learning from experience without forgetting prior training — across weights, context, and harnesses. Practical constraint for agentic AI deployment at scale.

### India AI/Tech
- **India AI gap — "giant sucking sound"** (The Ken / Rohin Dharmakumar): Indian businesses spent $18B on overseas acquisitions in the last year (+34% YoY); already $15B in 2026. But bold AI bets are not happening domestically. *"Why would India permit $30B of capital to flow abroad, contributing to rupee pressure?"* (Debashis Basu, Moneylife). India risks being a net capital exporter while not capturing AI value creation domestically.
- **AirTrunk $30B India data centers**: Data center operator commits to invest $30B in India — massive AI infrastructure commitment. If executed, one of the largest foreign infrastructure investments in India's history.
- **NXP Semiconductors bullish on India** as semiconductor and electronics manufacturing destination.
- **India signals stricter controls for AI and biometric devices** — evolving AI governance framework at policy level.

### Chip Manufacturing
- **Samsung Vietnam**: $4B chip-testing and packaging plant — $1.5B development + up to $2.5B in future earnings from the plant. Part of Samsung's strategy to diversify manufacturing footprint outside Korea and China.

### IPO / Funding
- **SpaceX IPO**: $25.5M legal bill filed; engaging top law firms. Investors rotating from Bitcoin ETFs to AI stocks and megacap IPO pipeline. SpaceX, Anthropic, OpenAI IPOs = defining 2026 financial markets narrative.

### Developer Tools
- **Claude dynamic workflows** (Linas newsletter): From first principles to production — every workflow pattern, cost trap, and use case for Anthropic's dynamic workflow capability. Most complex agentic patterns codified.
- **PM Skills 2.0**: 68 free Claude skills, 42 commands, 9 plugins — Claude Code ecosystem expanding. New /red-team-prd command and AI Shipping Kit.

## Signals

- **Google-SpaceX $920M/month deal** puts compute procurement at the scale of a sovereign infrastructure programme. India data center commitment (AirTrunk $30B) is the logical downstream: compute demand is a global arms race, and India is becoming a significant battlefield.
- **Samsung Vietnam** is another data point in the China+1 semiconductor supply chain strategy — Vietnam joining India, Malaysia, Mexico as manufacturing diversification destinations.
- **Anthropic's pause advocacy** changes the political economy of AI regulation: if the company that makes the most capable models calls for a coordination mechanism, regulators (EU, India, US) have both a policy hook and corporate backing to act.
- **India AI gap** is structurally concerning: $18B overseas acquisitions capital that isn't building AI competes with INR at a historic low. The capital-to-AI conversion rate for Indian corporates is lower than peers — policy design problem or risk appetite problem.
- **Bitcoin → AI equity rotation** is a capital market signal: speculative capital is reallocating from crypto to AI company equity in anticipation of Anthropic/OpenAI/SpaceX IPOs.
