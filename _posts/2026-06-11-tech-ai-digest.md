**a16z — "Living Context Layer" (a16z.com):**
- "The living context layer is being built inside companies right now, whether they're paying attention or not"

**Return on Tokens (ROT — Not Boring / Packy McCormick):**
- Framework: economic value of AI inference tokens relative to cost
- Company measurement lens: value generated per token vs. cost per token = ROT
- Implication: as inference gets cheaper (-80% since 2024), value-capture per token becomes the differentiating factor

**Benedict Evans annual AI report (The AI Corner):**

- Confirms we're past the initial hype peak; now entering "what's real vs. narrative" sorting phase

**Loop Engineering (Linas Beliunas):**

## Signals

- **Anthropic Mythos public + Fable 5 = new capability era entering mainstream** — when frontier models become publicly accessible (not just API), the adoption curve steepens. India enterprise AI adoption, which has lagged due to pricing and access barriers, could accelerate.
- **AI IPO race (Anthropic + OpenAI + global tech):** Capital markets window is brief — companies racing before AI hype cools or macro conditions tighten. For India: SEBI IPO pipeline shows same dynamic (Zepto, Kuku FM confidential). AI valuation premiums are real but time-limited.
- **NVIDIA-TSMC AI in fabs** is the meta-story: AI is now optimising its own manufacturing process. cuLitho/cuEST accelerate the pace of semiconductor design and production — compressing the innovation-to-production cycle. For India's chip manufacturing ambitions, this matters: India will build fabs using AI-accelerated tooling from day one, potentially narrowing the experience gap.
- **India Dholera first silicon (late 2026)** — if Tata delivers first silicon at 28nm on schedule, it's a credibility milestone for India Semiconductor Mission. The ecosystem (ASML deal + Micron ATMP already live + CG Power scaling) suggests the first-generation India semiconductor supply chain is real, not theoretical.
- **Living context layer** → India enterprise software implication: the first movers building ambient context capture inside Indian enterprises (BFSI, IT, healthcare) will own the AI productivity layer for a decade. Startups targeting this (RAG-on-enterprise-data, agentic memory, operational knowledge graphs) are in the right strategic position.
