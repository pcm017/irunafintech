# Just Dial Q1 FY27 Investor Presentation

## TL;DR

- Operating revenue ₹3,275 Mn (+9.9% YoY, +6.6% QoQ); operating EBITDA ₹874 Mn but margin compressed to 26.7% (from 29.0% YoY, -232bps).
- Net profit ₹1,663 Mn (+4.1% YoY, +66.2% QoQ) — the large QoQ jump is driven mainly by other income (₹1,315 Mn, +170.3% QoQ) rather than core operations.
- Cash & investments ₹60,221 Mn (+10.9% YoY) — a very large cash pile relative to quarterly operating revenue of ₹3,275 Mn, reflecting the company's asset-light, cash-generative local-search model.
- Total listings crossed 56.1 million (+13.0% YoY); paid campaigns 639,200 (+3.5% YoY); unique visitors roughly flat YoY at 192.9 million (-0.2%), with mobile share slightly declining (86.5%, from 86.9%).
- Total employees 13,337 (+2.1% YoY), with sales headcount growing faster (+7.8% YoY) than overall headcount — continued investment in feet-on-street sales capacity even as visitor growth plateaus.

## Signals

Just Dial's core operating metrics show a business past its growth-and-margin peak: EBITDA margin compressed 232bps YoY to 26.7% even as revenue grew a modest 9.9%, and unique visitors were flat-to-down YoY (-0.2%) despite continued listings growth (+13.0% YoY) — suggesting the company is adding supply-side inventory faster than it is growing demand-side traffic. The reported net profit growth (+4.1% YoY, +66.2% QoQ headline) is materially flattered by other income from its large ₹60,221 Mn cash/investment balance; on an operating basis (Operating PBT), the quarter was roughly flat YoY (+5.0%) and slightly down QoQ (-1.2%). This is the first Just Dial data point in the wiki — worth tracking against India's broader consumer-internet demand signals (Nykaa, Swiggy, Eternal) already logged, though Just Dial's B2B-listings model is structurally distinct from those consumer-transaction platforms.
