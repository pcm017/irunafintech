## Signals

1. **13-14 consecutive quarters of mid-20s GMV growth** is rare in Indian e-commerce. Nykaa's consistency signals that the India beauty market is expanding structurally, not just cyclically.

2. **Beauty EBITDA at 10.1% = mature business.** At 10%+ EBITDA margins with 27% GMV growth, Nykaa Beauty is the most profitable segment â€” and the growth rate is not decelerating meaningfully.

3. **Fashion improvement is the quarter's most important signal.** From âˆ’5.4% to âˆ’2.0% EBITDA margin in one year. Key driver: H&M brand win, improved customer acquisition ROI, reduced discounting. At this rate, Fashion approaches EBITDA breakeven in Q1/Q2 FY27.

4. **L'Oreal exclusive wins (La Roche-Posay, NYX, Kiehl's)** signal Nykaa has earned a trust position with the world's top beauty company that competitors cannot easily replicate. La Roche-Posay specifically (world #1 dermatologist-recommended skincare) entering India exclusively via Nykaa will drive significant skincare category GMV.

5. **eB2B (Superstore) at 485k retailers** across 1,100+ cities is an underappreciated asset â€” it's a distribution network for premium beauty brands into India's general and modern trade, at a scale that rivals dedicated FMCG distributors. As own-brand GMV grows, this becomes a high-margin owned-brand distribution play.
