### Gold import duty hike
> "Import tax on gold and silver has jumped from 6% to 15%, making them much more expensive in India. This comes as geopolitical tensions pushed oil and shipping costs up globally. With India's gold imports surging 24% to a record $71.9 billion this year, the government wants to reduce non-essential imports, conserve dollars, and protect the economy from rising trade deficit pressures." — Filter Coffee, May 14

**Market reactions:** Kalyan Jewellers, Titan, Senco Gold fell; Gold ETFs +15%; Muthoot Finance, Manappuram Finance gained; Hindustan Zinc surged.

### BMIP — Bharat Maritime Insurance Pool
> "India has launched a new $1.5 billion Bharat Maritime Insurance Pool to protect ships, cargo, and trade routes during wars, sanctions, or global geopolitical crises... The big thing here is the government's $1.4 billion sovereign guarantee." — Filter Coffee, May 14

Anthropic implied valuation: **$1.4 trillion** (up 40% in 24 days); annualized revenue $45B (from $100M to $45B in <3 years).

## Signals

**India macro:**
- INR weakening (95.65 from 94.44) — CAD and risk-off pressure confirming Hormuz impact
- CPI 3.48% is benign; but gold duty hike is a pre-emptive trade-deficit defense move
- Gold import duty hike signals government prioritising forex reserve conservation over consumer price stability in gold
- BMIP launch = government treating Hormuz disruption as a structural, not transient, risk that needs sovereign backstop

**BFSI sector:**
- Jio Financial JV with Allianz = serious entry into general insurance; Jio Financial is now credibly building across lending + insurance + payments; watch for impact on existing players (SBI General, Bajaj Allianz, HDFC ERGO)
- PSB hiring surge (24K in FY27) signals big banks expect volume growth, not contraction — counter to the AI-displacement narrative for India
- 10 fintechs preparing IPO = fintech liquidity wave incoming; watch for NSE/BSE filings

**Global AI / Anthropic:**
- Anthropic D&B: 580M business entity database natively in Claude = KYC/KYB/credit risk for banks and fintechs directly through Claude API; RegTech middleware vendors (Jumio, Onfido, Bureau.id, Perfios) face displacement risk
- Anthropic at $1.4T implied valuation: the SPV secondary market has become so heated that Anthropic has declared 8 firms as selling void shares. Pre-IPO compliance posture.

**DeFi / crypto:**
- Sky/MakerDAO is now effectively a money market fund — the DeFi-TradFi convergence is real and happening at scale ($8.245B RWA)
- On-chain vs. off-chain regulatory arbitrage will close: if Sky is a money market fund, SEC/RBI regulatory treatment will follow
