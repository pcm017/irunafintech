## Signals

**Change:** US-Iran draft truce triggered a coordinated global risk-on rally — every major equity index up 1.6–2.8%; Brent -4% extending the June 11 deescalation signal. This is the second consecutive session of geopolitical premium unwinding following Trump's June 11 attack suspension.

**Root Cause:** Back-channel Iran deal framework reportedly progressing. RBI's coordinated capital inflow package (FCNR(B) hedging subsidy + PSU ECB swap + G-Sec tax exemption) is attracting NRI and foreign portfolio inflows simultaneously — INR ~85.76 is the combined result of these flows and falling crude import pressure.

**Sizing / India Impact:** Brent at $89 (vs. $111 peak in May) already represents a ~$6–8B/month reduction in India's energy import bill. A formal deal closing could push Brent toward $75–80, implying a further ~$4–5B/month savings. INR appreciation from 95+ to 85.76 directly lowers fuel prices, CPI, and RBI's constraint on rate cuts. This is India's single largest macro windfall scenario of 2026.

**Secondary signal — EU-China trade war building:** G7 France (June 16–17) and EU summit Brussels dedicated to countering China. "Smart money says Europe more talk than action this summer, but that destination [trade war] may be inevitable" — Sharp Text. India could benefit as EU trade policy shifts away from China (electronics, EV components, green tech supply chains).
