## Signals

- **Anthropic at $965B valuation** represents roughly 20x of its ~$50B ARR (if The Ken's $45B+ ARR estimate from May 14 is directionally correct) — extraordinary for a product-era company; the question is whether model revenue compounds at this pace or plateaus; the $65B raise likely gives Anthropic 5+ years of runway to push toward GPT-like dominance
- **Mythos model** — the existence of a model significant enough to alarm central bank security teams suggests Anthropic is working on capabilities beyond current public release; this has enterprise cybersecurity and financial services implications
- **Intel + 3DGS substrate tech to India** is structurally more significant than the headline suggests — substrate manufacturing is a chokepoint in chip packaging; India currently does only ATMP (assembly/test); adding substrate capability moves India one step up the semiconductor value chain
- **Orbital data centres (Agnikul)** — if viable, orbital infrastructure for AI/data storage would be a sovereign capability with defence and strategic implications; early stage but India-relevant
- **AI venture era ending narrative** aligns with the meta-trend: Anthropic's raise is the exclamation point on the AI funding boom; the next phase is about who can generate real revenue at scale; the ecosystem will bifurcate between profitable AI companies and those facing valuation resets
