## Signals

- **S&P/Nasdaq at records while India sells off** — India-US divergence persists but oil declining helps the trajectory; key watchpoint: does Nifty catch up if Iran deal confirms?
- **COT crude near net-short flip:** For the first time since 2008, managed money is abandoning crude longs — signals Iran deal is being priced in. If deal materialises, this becomes a forced short-covering rally.
- **EUR/USD net-long at Dec 2023 high:** Dollar softening thesis building — 5 consecutive weeks of rising EUR longs. USD weakness would help EM and INR.
- **Three Fs framework:** FM Sitharaman's public framing of risk vectors is a policy signal — government is in active crisis management mode, not dismissing the shock.
- **CRISIL 200 bps margin warning:** First quantified estimate of West Asia impact on Indian corporate earnings. If FY27 corporate earnings show 200 bps margin compression, equity valuations need re-rating downward.
- **India defense indigenisation:** Fighter jet shortlist (Tata, L&T, Bharat Forge) signals major domestic manufacturing opportunity and strategic shift.
- **Net FDI crisis:** Gross FDI steady but profit repatriation rising — net FDI declining. Structural concern for current account management.
