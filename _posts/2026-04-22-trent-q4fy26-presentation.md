## TL;DR

- Q4FY26 standalone revenue ₹4,937 Cr (+20% YoY), EBITDA ₹668 Cr (+43%), operating EBIT ₹567 Cr (+43%), PAT ₹455 Cr (+30%) — profit growth well ahead of revenue growth, signalling operating leverage.
- Consolidated (incl. JVs/associates): revenue ₹5,028 Cr (+19%), EBITDA ₹653 Cr (+44%), EBIT ₹543 Cr (+42%), PAT ₹413 Cr (+33%).
- Store network: 1,286 stores across 321 cities (incl. 3 in UAE), 17.70 Mn sq. ft. retail area — Westside 300 stores, Zudio 963 stores, Star (food & grocery) 84 stores (incl. Zudio-in-Star format).
- Board approved in-principle an additional capital raise of ~₹2,500 Cr to fund store upgrades, new brands/categories/geographies, supply-chain automation, and Star's real-estate-backed store rollout.
- FY26 ROCE >29%; like-for-like fashion growth in low single digits for both Q4 and full year, reflecting a deliberate strategy of expanding into new micro-markets (Zudio: >80% of new stores in Tier II/III cities) rather than maximising same-store growth.

## Key data / quotes

**Q4FY26 standalone highlights:** Revenue from operations ₹4,937 Cr (+20%), EBITDA ₹668 Cr (+43%), Op. EBIT ₹567 Cr (+43%), PAT ₹455 Cr (+30%).

**Q4FY26 consolidated (incl. JV/associate share):** Revenue ₹5,028 Cr (+19%), EBITDA ₹653 Cr (+44%), Op. EBIT ₹543 Cr (+42%), PAT ₹413 Cr (+33%).

**Store portfolio (as of 31 Mar 2026):** Westside 300 stores (7.26 Mn+ sq ft, 97 cities); Zudio 963 stores incl. 6 in UAE (10.38 Mn+ sq ft, 313 cities); Star/food & grocery 84 stores (1.44 Mn+ sq ft, 12 cities), 73% own-brand share. In Q4FY26 alone: 23 Westside + 109 Zudio store openings (incl. 2 UAE); full year: 60 Westside + 212 Zudio openings (incl. 4 UAE).

**Management commentary (Results Context):** "Consumer sentiment was relatively stable at the beginning of Q4. The impact of the unfolding geopolitical situation is still playing out. Consumers are spending with caution, resulting in moderation of discretionary spending." Input costs on select raw materials beginning to show inflationary pressure; predominant sourcing remains India-based. "In FY26, our ROCE was over 29%." Capital raise: "Board considered and approved in-principle a proposal for raising additional funds of c. Rs. 2500 crores" for store upgrades, new brand/category/geography incubation, supply-chain automation, and Star real-estate-backed rollout.

## Signals

Trent's Q4FY26 profit growth (EBITDA +43%, PAT +30%) running well ahead of its revenue growth (+20%) is the clearest operating-leverage signal in this quarter's print — store-count expansion (1,286 stores, +109 Zudio additions in the quarter alone) is being absorbed without proportional cost growth, consistent with management's commentary on RFID-driven supply-chain productivity and manpower-cost containment. The ~₹2,500 Cr capital raise, flagged as "in-principle" with no timeline, signals continued aggressive network expansion (Zudio's >80% Tier II/III store mix) even as like-for-like growth stays in the low single digits — Trent is prioritising reach/share-of-market over same-store comp growth, a strategy choice rather than a demand-weakness signal. Management's own caution ("consumers spending with caution... moderation of discretionary spending" amid "unfolding geopolitical situation") is a rare direct read-through from India's fashion/retail sector on the same West Asia-conflict consumer-sentiment channel tracked elsewhere in this wiki's macro pages — worth watching alongside India CPI/consumption data for corroboration or divergence.
