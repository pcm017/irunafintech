## TL;DR

Q4 FY26 confirms the credit cost normalisation story — provisions fell sharply to ₹2,008 Cr (from ₹3,425 Cr in Q3 FY26), driving PAT to ₹5,553 Cr (+36.6% QoQ, +22.2% YoY). AUM crossed ₹5.1 lakh crore at ₹5,09,975 Cr (+22.4% YoY). Full year FY26 PAT: ₹19,332 Cr (+15.2% YoY). AI transformation is tracking: 27 autonomous agents deployed, developer efficiency 28.5%, AI-generated disbursals ₹1,895 Cr in Q4.
