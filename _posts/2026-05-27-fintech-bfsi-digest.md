## Key Data / Quotes

**DCB Bank:** Recalibrating workforce, shifting away from DSA networks. Implications: distribution model shift across mid-sized private banks as digital channels mature.

**100% FDI in insurance:** Government decision; multiple insurers evaluating foreign capital raise. Key potential beneficiaries: insurers with existing foreign JV partners (Aviva, Allianz/Jio, Prudential/ICICI Prudential).

**RBI dividend (₹2.87 lakh crore):** Specifically highlighted as the mechanism enabling the government to absorb fuel excise cuts (forgoing ₹14,000 Cr) and OMC support without expanding fiscal deficit.

**Vi credit rating upgrade:** Distressed telco with government bailout improving creditor confidence — potentially eases near-term refinancing risk.

**BSNL ₹25,000 Cr revenue; Starlink partnership:** If Starlink partnership materialises, significant for rural broadband connectivity — both strategic (defence/borders) and commercial (underserved India coverage).

**SEBI ban:** 7 individuals banned in coordinated ₹20 Cr stock manipulation case — enforcement continuing.

**RBI UCB norms:** Tightened re-appointment standards for Urban Co-operative Bank board directors — governance reform in the co-op banking sector (follows Sarvodaya licence revocation).

**SIDBI Fund of Funds:** Rs 10,000 Cr programme over 10 years; now launching FoF 2.0. Key tensions: "professional, pricey, and playing favourites" — some VCs describe as structured and instrumental; others cite friction, politics, economics as barriers. Significant as India's primary domestic LP in VC.

**Monzo £1.7B revenue:** Still geographically concentrated in the UK — international expansion is the next challenge for the digital bank.

**F6S India startup funding:** Week's total ₹5,034 Cr (down 37%); AI funding moving back to early-stage future unicorns. India 2026 YTD: $7.36B across 743 rounds (−11.91% vs 2025).

---

## Signals

- **Insurance FDI liberalisation:** 100% FDI in insurance is a structural opening. Could attract large global insurers (AXA, Allianz, Prudential, Zurich) to take controlling positions. Watch Jio Allianz (already 49/49/2 structure), HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz re-negotiations.
- **DCB Bank distribution shift:** Broader industry signal — mid-sized banks de-risking DSA dependency. Positive for direct/digital channel efficiency; short-term revenue pressure from distribution gap.
- **BSNL-Starlink:** Strategic significance beyond commercial — Starlink connectivity to BSNL's rural towers would leapfrog infrastructure constraints. Watch for Trai/DoT approval process.
- **SIDBI FoF 2.0:** Continued government backstop for early-stage VC in India. The "pricey but professional" debate reflects the cost of government capital (reporting, compliance, selection). For emerging fund managers, SIDBI LP status provides validation but at a compliance premium.
- **Monzo scale ceiling:** £1.7B revenue but UK-concentrated — applies to all India neobanks too: domestic scale is achievable but geographic expansion requires regulatory navigation.
- **India funding rotation to early-stage:** Later-stage rounds (Series B+) slowing; early-stage AI startups attracting attention. Consistent with F6S data showing YTD decline vs 2025.
