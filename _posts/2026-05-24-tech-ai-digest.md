**Signals**
- "End of free compute" thesis is now validated by real corporate data: Uber's budget exhaustion and Microsoft's Claude Code rollback are the first major enterprise AI cost-discipline signals. Token constraint will force smarter deployment decisions — **outcome-based AI metrics will replace token volume metrics** in 18–24 months
- Anthropic's **legal vertical** is becoming the largest non-coding enterprise AI category: 20+ MCP connectors, 12 plugins, Spotdraft as a system-of-record layer. Legal is the first regulated profession being rebuilt around Claude
- IBM's join signals enterprise credibility for Anthropic's cybersecurity positioning — moves beyond developer/coding into Fortune 500 CIO-level relationships
- Google's 4-year AGI countdown from a lab CEO is the most significant public forecast in AI history — a deliberate market signal (vs. OpenAI's implied urgency, Anthropic's safety-first positioning)
- Tech Mahindra's GPUaaS-for-telcos thesis is India-specific: BSNL/Jio tower infrastructure as distributed AI inference nodes — a new infrastructure monetisation pathway
