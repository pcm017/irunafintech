## Signals

- **Nasdaq -4.18%** is the market's response to better-than-expected jobs data — higher employment → higher-for-longer rates → risk assets repriced lower. Semiconductor sector bore the brunt (AI capex valuation multiples most sensitive to rate outlook).
- **Gold at 2026 lows** despite geopolitical tension is a signal: the jobs report overrode all safe-haven demand. If US rates stay elevated, gold's uptrend reverses structurally.
- **RBI's capital inflow package** is a direct response to INR weakness and CAD pressure — LCR-linked pricing, FII G-Sec tax exemption, and neutral stance together signal RBI is trying to attract bond market FII without hiking rates.
- **Sahamati SRO for AA ecosystem** is structural — formalises the governance layer needed for India's account aggregation to scale from pilot to production.
- **COT: WTI net short** (~26,694) + **crude at 15-year bearish extreme among managed money** = contrarian signal for potential oil price recovery if geopolitical tensions remain.
- **KOSPI ▼5.54%** is an outlier among Asian markets — likely Samsung/semiconductor-specific.
