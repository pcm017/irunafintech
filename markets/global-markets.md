## Thesis / View

**New regime driver — US rate expectations:** The US May jobs report has shifted the dominant market narrative from geopolitical risk to rate-path expectations. A strong jobs report delays Fed cuts; higher-for-longer US rates = Nasdaq/AI equity compression, gold selling, and compressed EM carry. This is the first major rate-expectation-driven selloff since the Iran conflict began.

**Two-sided India trade:** India is simultaneously a beneficiary (FDI +18%, capital inflow measures, FII G-Sec exemption) and a victim (INR pressure, WPI 8.3%, CRISIL 200bps margin dent) of current macro conditions. The RBI June 5 package ($5B/month target) + FII G-Sec ordinance is attempting to address the CAD financing gap without rate hikes — the data over next 4–6 weeks (FII flows, May CPI on June 12, June trade deficit) will show if it works.

**Gold at 2026 low — positioning divergence.** COT gold net long jumped to +176,000 (+21,700 WoW) even as gold price hit $4,342 (2026 low). If speculators are right, this is a buy-the-dip moment with geopolitical protection. If wrong, forced liquidation of 176K contracts could push gold below $4,000. The jobs-report rate shock vs. geopolitical bid is the defining tension for gold in 2H 2026.

**WTI net short at 15-year extremes = structural contrarian signal.** Managed money has not been this bearish on crude since 2008. If Hormuz escalates (Iran, US-Iran re-flare) or the Iran deal fails entirely, a violent short-squeeze is structurally possible.

**Domestic floor intact:** Bank credit 16.2% YoY (fastest in 2 years) + UPI 23.2B txns/month + SIP flows absorbed FII selling = domestic macro is holding despite external shocks. Nifty unlikely to break below 22,500 as long as DII SIP flows ₹25,000+ Cr/month persist.

**Rate path:** India-US 10Y spread compressed to ~246bps (from 252bps prior). RBI in hold mode with fiscal/bond-market tools (not rate moves) as the primary response instrument. Watch May CPI (due ~June 12) — if below 4%, a rate cut at August MPC becomes a conversation.
