## Patterns

- **Transactional bilateralism:** Every trade deal is structured as a direct exchange (India lower tariffs + stop Russian oil = US lower tariffs). No multilateral framing.
- **Immigration-finance linkage:** Using banking system as an enforcement mechanism for immigration policy — a new and expanding playbook.
- **China engagement oscillation:** Tariff war → partial truce (Oct 2025) → Beijing summit (May 2026). Volatility likely to continue.
- **Legal limits emerging:** Federal courts now actively constraining executive tariff authority; creates policy uncertainty across all trade relationships.
