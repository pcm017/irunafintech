# Indian Bank

**NSE/BSE:** INDIANB | 532814 | **Type:** Public Sector Bank

## One-liner

Indian Bank is a public-sector bank with a ₹15.29 lakh crore business base, distinguished in Q1 FY27 by among the cleanest asset-quality metrics in the PSU bank space (GNPA 1.86%, PCR 98.22%) and a strategy of hiring AI talent even as several private-sector peers cut headcount.

---

## Current State

*As of Q1 FY27 (quarter ending June 30, 2026):*

Indian Bank delivered broad-based double-digit growth: **net profit ₹3,273 Cr (+10% YoY, +5% QoQ)**, **operating profit ₹5,557 Cr (+17% YoY)**, **NII ₹7,435 Cr (+17% YoY)**. Business crossed **₹15.29 lakh Cr (+13.66% YoY)** with deposits and advances both growing ~13.5-14% YoY. Asset quality improved sharply: **GNPA 1.86% (-115bps YoY)**, **NNPA 0.15% (-3bps YoY)**, **PCR 98.22%** — among the strongest coverage ratios logged for any bank in this wiki. RoA 1.31%, RoE 19.48% (+78bps YoY). Separately, per the 2026-07-14 fintech-bfsi digest, Indian Bank is hiring 20 AI experts in the same cycle that saw top private banks (HDFC Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank) collectively cut 7,700+ employees in FY26 — a concrete PSU-vs-private divergence in how banks are approaching AI-era restructuring. See *2026 07 10 indian bank q1fy27 presentation*, *2026 07 14 fintech bfsi digest*

## Thesis / View

Indian Bank enters the wiki with an unusually strong asset-quality profile for a PSU bank (GNPA 1.86%, PCR 98.22%) — comparable to or better than several private banks tracked elsewhere in this wiki. The AI-hiring signal (20 experts, same cycle as private-bank layoffs) is worth watching as a potential leading indicator: if Indian Bank is investing in AI capability from strength while private banks cut costs defensively, it could signal a shift in competitive dynamics within Indian banking's AI transition, though one data point is not yet a trend.
