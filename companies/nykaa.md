# Nykaa (FSN E-Commerce Ventures Limited)

**One-liner:** India's dominant online beauty and fashion marketplace, with 276 physical stores, 18.7M annual transacting customers, and a growing portfolio of own consumer brands ($400M annualised GMV).

---

## Current State

*As of Q3 FY26 (quarter ending December 31, 2025)*

Nykaa has delivered 13–14 consecutive quarters of mid-20s GMV growth — a rare sustained trajectory in Indian e-commerce. Q3 FY26 (seasonally strong OND quarter) marked Nykaa's highest-ever EBITDA margin. The company is now in transition from a pure marketplace to an omnichannel retailer + brand-builder + distribution platform + GCC expansion play.

**Q3 FY26 headline results:**
- GMV: **₹5,795 Cr** (+28% YoY)
- Net Revenue: **₹2,873 Cr** (+27% YoY)
- Gross Profit: **₹1,297 Cr** (45.2% of NR, +31% YoY)
- EBITDA: **₹230 Cr** (8.0% of NR, +63% YoY) — **highest ever margin**
- PAT: **₹68 Cr** (2.4% of NR, +156% YoY) [adjusted for new labour code one-time: ₹78 Cr / 2.7%]

**By segment:**
- Beauty GMV: ₹4,302 Cr (+27% YoY); Beauty EBITDA margin: 10.1%
- Fashion GMV: ₹1,476 Cr (+31% YoY); Fashion EBITDA margin: −2.0% (vs. −5.4% YoY — sharp improvement)
- 9M FY26: Total GMV ₹14,682 Cr (Beauty ₹11,062 Cr +27%, Fashion ₹3,620 Cr +31%)

**Distribution footprint:**
- Physical stores: **276 stores** across **94 cities** (~2.8 lakh sq ft)
- Formats: Nykaa Luxe, Nykaa On Trend, Nykaa Kiosk, Nykaa Perfumery (new fragrance-only), Kay Kafe
- Superstore (eB2B): 485,000+ retailers, 1,100+ cities
- Annual unique transacting customers: **18.7M** (+26% YoY)

**Brand portfolio:**
- Consumer brands: 12 significant brands (Dot & Key, Kay Beauty, Nykaa Cosmetics, Nykd, Twenty Dresses + 7 others)
- Combined annualised GMV: **$400M** across own brands; 16M+ customers served
- Key international exclusives: La Roche-Posay (exclusive India launch Q3), NYX Makeup (exclusive distribution), Kiehl's (end-to-end operations), Milk Makeup, Kylie Cosmetics, Dolce Gabbana, H&M Beauty
- L'Oreal partnership: decade-long, deepened in Q3 with 3 new brands

**GCC (International):**
- Nysaa (GCC platform): ₹1,980 Cr annualised premium*, growing strongly
- *Note: ₹1,980 Cr annualised figure sourced from PB Fintech reference; may not apply directly to Nykaa GCC; to be validated against Nykaa-specific disclosures

## Thesis / View

**Nykaa's durable growth advantage is India's beauty underpenetration.** India's per-capita beauty spend is $14 vs. $300+ in developed markets and $130+ in China. With 1B+ population, growing urban middle class, and rising female workforce participation, the TAM is massive. Nykaa has 18.7M annual transacting customers — penetration into the addressable Indian beauty consumer (200M+) is still low single digits.

**The brand portfolio is the long-term value layer.** $400M annualised GMV from own brands (Dot & Key, Kay Beauty, Nykd, etc.) at higher margins than marketplace GMV is the compounding engine. As own-brand GMV grows from ~7% of total ($400M of $5.7B annualised) toward 15–20%, group margins will structurally improve.

**Omnichannel is necessary, not sufficient.** 276 stores across 94 cities creates brand visibility (discovery → online purchase) and customer trust, but also increases fixed costs. The health of the physical business depends on maintaining like-for-like growth (double-digit per management in Q3) while managing cost per sq ft. Nykaa Perfumery (new format) is the high-risk, high-upside bet on fragrance as an underpenetrated category.

**Fashion profitability path is the investment thesis test.** Fashion EBITDA margin improved from −5.4% (Q3 FY25) to −2.0% (Q3 FY26) — 340 bps improvement in one year. At current trajectory, Fashion could approach EBITDA breakeven in 2-3 quarters. Full profitability (positive EBITDA) in Fashion would make Nykaa's overall profitability profile dramatically better.

**L'Oreal partnership deepening = moat signal.** A decade-long partnership with the world's largest beauty company, now extended to La Roche-Posay (world's #1 dermatologist recommended skincare), NYX, and Kiehl's full-stack ops, confirms Nykaa's role as the preferred India channel for premium international beauty brands. This creates a virtuous cycle: exclusive launches → customer acquisition → data on premium buyers → more exclusive brands → higher ASPs.

## Contradictions

- **Fashion GMV vs. NSV divergence.** Fashion GMV +31% but NSV +25% (9M FY26). 600 bps gap suggests discounting or returns. Management acknowledged this gap exists but did not fully explain it on the earnings call. Monitoring for improvement in NSV/GMV ratio as a proxy for discount pressure.

- **GCC (Nysaa) revenue:** A ₹1,980 Cr annualised figure for GCC appeared in PB Fintech reference material, not directly in Nykaa's Q3 FY26 disclosure. Cross-check needed against Q4 FY26 or direct Nykaa disclosure before treating as confirmed.
