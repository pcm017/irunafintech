# Just Dial Limited

**NSE/BSE:** JUSTDIAL | 535648 | **Sector:** Local search / consumer internet

## One-liner

Just Dial is India's local-search and business-listings platform (56.1 million listings, 192.9 million unique monthly visitors) — an asset-light, highly cash-generative business (₹60,221 Mn cash & investments) whose core operating margins are compressing even as revenue grows modestly.

---

## Current State

*As of Q1 FY27 (quarter ending June 30, 2026):*

Just Dial's operating revenue grew **+9.9% YoY to ₹327.5 Cr**, but **operating EBITDA margin compressed 232bps YoY to 26.7%** — the core business is decelerating on profitability even as top-line grows. Reported **net profit ₹166.3 Cr (+4.1% YoY, +66.2% QoQ)** is materially flattered by other income (a 170% QoQ jump in investment income off the company's large cash pile); on an operating basis, PBT was roughly flat YoY and down 1.2% QoQ. Unique visitors were flat-to-down YoY (-0.2% to 192.9 Mn) despite listings growing +13.0% YoY to 56.1 million — supply-side inventory is outpacing demand-side traffic growth. Cash & investments stand at **₹60,221 Mn (+10.9% YoY)**, a very large balance relative to quarterly revenue, underscoring the business's asset-light, cash-generative structure even as growth matures.
