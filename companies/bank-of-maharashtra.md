# Bank of Maharashtra

**NSE/BSE:** 532525 | **Type:** Public Sector Bank

## One-liner

Bank of Maharashtra is a Pune-headquartered public-sector bank that posted one of the strongest PSU bank results logged in this wiki for Q1 FY27 — net profit +26.84% YoY, total business +19.10% YoY (well above system-average credit growth), and improving asset quality (GNPA 1.45%, PCR 98.55%).

---

## Current State

*As of Q1 FY27 (quarter ending June 30, 2026):*

Bank of Maharashtra delivered **net profit ₹2,020 Cr (+26.84% YoY)** and **operating profit ₹3,117 Cr (+21.29% YoY)**, with **NII up 14.53% YoY**. **Total business reached ₹6,50,457 Cr (+19.10% YoY)** — running well ahead of the 17.7% YoY system-wide bank credit growth logged in the 2026-07-14 macro-signals snapshot; **global advances grew 26.90% YoY to ₹3,05,964 Cr**. Asset quality continued improving: **GNPA 1.45% (from 1.74% YoY)**, **NNPA 0.13% (from 0.18% YoY)**, **PCR 98.55%**. **RoA improved to 1.90%** (from 1.80% YoY). The one caution signal: **CASA ratio fell to 49%** (from 53% at Q4 FY26) even as term deposits grew a strong +16.62% YoY — a mix shift toward costlier funding that bears watching against future NIM trends.
