# L&T Finance Limited (LTF)

**NSE/BSE:** LTF | 533519 | **Parent:** Larsen & Toubro Group

## One-liner

L&T Finance is a retail-focused upper-layer NBFC (part of the L&T Group) with a ₹1.3 lakh crore book across ~2,00,000 villages and 450+ cities/towns, executing a "Lakshya 2031" strategic plan targeting 20%+ book growth, <2% credit cost, 3.0-3.2% ROA, and 16-18% ROE by FY31 as an "AI-native, risk-first, tech-first" retail financial services institution.

---

## Current State

*As of Q1 FY27 (quarter ending June 30, 2026):*

L&T Finance's book grew to **₹1,29,634 Cr (+27% YoY, +6% QoQ)** — running ahead of its own Lakshya 2031 target of 20%+ annual book growth. **PAT ₹902 Cr (+29% YoY, +12% QoQ)**; **ROA 2.48%** and **ROE 12.71%**, both improving steadily (+11bps and +185bps YoY respectively) but still below the FY31 targets of 3.0-3.2% ROA and 16-18% ROE. Credit cost held at **2.54%** (after macro-prudential provision utilization), an improvement from 3.43% pre-utilization a year ago. Retail asset quality improved: Stage 3 (GNPA-equivalent) at 2.48% of the retail book, down from 2.93% a year ago; Net Stage 3 0.77%. Disbursements ₹23,852 Cr (+36% YoY) though flat-to-down QoQ (-1%). The one caution flag: NIM dipped to 8.54% (from 8.78% QoQ) even as leverage rose (Debt/Equity 4.09x, up from 3.93x QoQ) — a combination worth monitoring for whether growth is becoming more leverage-dependent. ## Thesis / View
